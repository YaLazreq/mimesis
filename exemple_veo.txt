SCÈNE 1 — Hook / L'Éveil
Macro shot, fixed on tripod, slight breathing movement, 
pull focus from linen fabric to bag as hand enters frame. 9: 16 vertical.

A Jacquemus Bambino Long bag in beige/sand leather rests at the center 
of crumpled white linen sheets, slightly angled. A feminine arm enters 
the frame and gently grazes the leather surface.
The bag is closed, bandoulière forming an elegant curve on the sheets. 
Jacquemus logo subtly visible.
Using provided reference image for product consistency: 
same grainy leather texture, same sand-beige tone (#E8D5B7), 
same embossed logo throughout.

Setting: Mediterranean bedroom, raw white plaster walls, 
aged wooden shutters, low bed with natural linen, 
terracotta tile floor.

Lighting: Dawn golden light, directional warm light (3200K) 
entering through wooden shutters, casting sharp horizontal shadow 
lines across the sheets. Dust particles visible in the light beam. 
Soft contrast.

Style: Fantasized Mediterranean. Natural materials palette — 
linen, stone, wood, terracotta. Warm sand tones (#F5E6D3, #E8C4A0). 
Subtle 35mm film grain, Kodak Portra 400 look. 
Warm shadows, slightly washed highlights.
References: Luca Guadagnino films, early Jacquemus campaigns, 
Viviane Sassen photography.
Keywords: golden hour, leather texture, cut shadows, 
Mediterranean, sensual minimalism, amber light.

Music: Mediterranean modern acoustic — fingerpicking guitar 
blended with light electronic beats, slow and intimate opening.
SFX: Soft leather sound as fingertips brush the bag surface.
Ambient: Early morning silence, distant birds, 
light breeze through wooden shutters.
Emotion: Curiosity, mystery.

No voiceover. No text overlay. No jump cuts. 
No artificial lighting. Photorealistic. 9: 16 vertical.

SCÈNE 2 — Context / Le Monde du Sac
Reverse tracking shot, American framing from hips, 
slight low angle, gimbal stabilized, fluid movement. 9: 16 vertical.

A Bambino Long bag worn cross-body against the right hip, 
against a white linen dress. The natural swinging motion 
of the walk draws the eye. The leather catches the light 
in bright zones of the alley.
Only the bag and the hips are visible — no face.
Using provided reference image for product consistency: 
same grainy leather texture, same sand-beige tone (#E8D5B7), 
same embossed logo throughout.

Setting: Narrow Provençal village alley — type Gordes or Bonnieux. 
Ochre and pale yellow aged walls, old irregular stone pavement, 
geometric shadows from balconies and shutters. 
Blurred market in the background. Jasmine climbing the walls.

Lighting: Warm morning light, not yet intense. 
Alternating zones of bright light and cool shadow 
as the figure moves through the alley.

Style: Fantasized Mediterranean. Natural materials — 
stone, plaster, sun-baked earth. Warm sand tones (#F5E6D3, #E8C4A0). 
Subtle 35mm film grain, Kodak Portra 400 look.
References: Luca Guadagnino films, early Jacquemus campaigns.
Keywords: cut shadows, Mediterranean, freedom, 
slow motion, amber light, sensual minimalism.

Music: Acoustic guitar picking up pace slightly, 
the rhythm matching the walk.
SFX: Slow footsteps on old stone pavement, 
subtle bag swaying sound.
Ambient: Provençal village morning — 
distant market murmur, cicadas beginning.
Emotion: Escape, freedom.

No voiceover. No text overlay. No jump cuts. 
No artificial lighting. Photorealistic. 9: 16 vertical.

SCÈNE 3 — Product Entry / La Révélation
Extreme macro shot, fixed,
50mm macro lens. 
Rack focus from fingers to bag interior to peach. 
Slight slow motion 48fps. 9: 16 vertical.

Natural-nailed, lightly tanned hands open the magnetic clasp 
of the Bambino Long with a clean, precise gesture. 
The honey-colored suede interior is revealed. 
A hand slowly reaches inside and pulls out a perfect, 
velvety ripe peach — slow, almost sensual movement.
The magnetic click must be visible and audible.
Using provided reference image for product consistency: 
same grainy leather texture, same sand-beige tone (#E8D5B7), 
same embossed logo, honey suede interior.

Setting: Shot so close the background is full bokeh — 
creamy blur of blue sky and olive green. 
No setting detail visible.

Lighting: Soft overhead light, diffused, like midday sun 
under a canvas parasol. Warm but not harsh. 
Micro-reflections on the grainy leather surface.

Style: Product as absolute hero. Artisanal quality is everything.
Warm sand tones (#F5E6D3, #E8C4A0, #D4A574). 
Subtle 35mm film grain. Creamy bokeh.
References: Luca Guadagnino tactile close-ups, 
Viviane Sassen bold compositions.
Keywords: leather texture, magnetic click, 
suede interior, sensual gesture, macro, desire.

Music: The beat drops slightly — one suspended note, 
guitar string held.
SFX: Crisp magnetic clasp click. 
Soft rustle of suede as bag opens. 
Slight sound of peach being lifted.
Ambient: Near silence. A single bee in the distance. 
Leaves in a light breeze.
Emotion: Desire, fascination.

No voiceover. No text overlay. No jump cuts. 
No artificial lighting. Photorealistic. 9: 16 vertical.

SCÈNE 4 — Transformation / L'Immersion
Fixed shot, slight overhead angle, natural gaze. 
Very slow micro-dolly forward (2cm over 5 seconds). 
Light breeze animates the bougainvillea. 9: 16 vertical.

The Bambino Long bag rests open and relaxed on a 
patinated wrought iron table. A glass of pale rosé 
sits beside it, condensation on the glass. 
A cascade of fuchsia bougainvillea in the background. 
Geometric shadow patterns from a wooden trellis 
fall across the table and the bag.
Using provided reference image for product consistency: 
same grainy leather texture, same sand-beige tone (#E8D5B7), 
clasp and logo visible.

Setting: Village café terrace. Printed cement tiles, 
white walls, wooden trellis with bougainvillea, 
rattan chair. Dolce Vita atmosphere.

Lighting: Warm afternoon light filtered through trellis. 
Geometric shadow patterns on the table and bag. 
Golden warmth. Siesta atmosphere.

Style: Fantasized Mediterranean still life. 
Everything feels found, not staged.
Color palette: fuchsia of bougainvillea against 
sand tones (#F5E6D3, #E8C4A0). 
Subtle 35mm film grain, Kodak Portra 400.
References: Early Jacquemus campaigns, 
Viviane Sassen color saturations.
Keywords: golden hour, cut shadows, Mediterranean, 
nature morte vivante, slow motion, amber light.

Music: Guitar and light electronic beats, 
relaxed mid-section — the commercial breathes here.
SFX: Ice against glass as rosé is set down. 
Light wind through bougainvillea petals.
Ambient: Café terrace — distant conversation, 
ceramic cups, lazy afternoon heat.
Emotion: Fullness, quiet joy.

No voiceover. No text overlay. No jump cuts. 
No artificial lighting. Photorealistic. 9: 16 vertical.

SCÈNE 5 — Climax / L'Heure Dorée
Medium shot, backlight, slow crane movement 
from low to high revealing the horizon progressively. 
Natural lens flare from the setting sun. 
Slow motion 60fps. 9: 16 vertical.

The Bambino Long held by its bandoulière at arm's length, 
feminine silhouette in chinese shadow. The golden sunset 
light wraps entirely around the leather, 
making it glow like amber gold. 
The Mediterranean sea shimmers with orange reflections behind.
Using provided reference image for product consistency: 
same grainy leather texture catching the golden light, 
same sand-beige leather now lit amber (#C4956A in this light).

Setting: Rocky cliff or promontoire facing the Mediterranean. 
Dry grass in the foreground. Clear horizon, 
sky gradient orange-pink-violet. 
Turquoise water turning golden.

Lighting: Full golden hour — 2800K. 
Artistic backlight. The leather acts as a golden reflector. 
Luminous halo around the silhouette. 
Natural lens flare from the sun.

Style: Maximum visual spectacle — the emotional peak.
Warm amber palette (#C4956A, #D4A574, #F5E6D3). 
Subtle 35mm film grain. Halo and flare.
References: Luca Guadagnino golden hour, 
Viviane Sassen contre-jour.
Keywords: golden hour, amber light, Mediterranean, 
slow motion, beauty, wonder, contre-jour.

Music: The music reaches its peak — 
guitar and electronic beat at full swell, 
emotional crescendo.
SFX: Wind carrying the bandoulière. 
Distant waves breaking on rocks below.
Ambient: Coastal golden hour — wind, sea, music swells.
Emotion: Wonder, pure beauty.

No voiceover. No text overlay. No jump cuts. 
No artificial lighting. Photorealistic. 9: 16 vertical.

SCÈNE 6 — Resolution / La Signature
Fixed shot, perfectly frontal and symmetrical. 
Slight plan breathing. Slow fade to black (1.5s) 
toward end card. 9: 16 vertical.

The Bambino Long sits alone on a white stone ledge 
worn smooth by sea salt. The last light of the day 
creates a warm halo around the bag. 
The sea is calm, deep blue turning dark.
The bag is treated as a sculpture on a pedestal — 
the last image before the end card.
Using provided reference image for product consistency: 
same grainy leather texture, same sand-beige tone (#E8D5B7), 
same embossed logo perfectly centered and readable.

Setting: White stone ledge facing the sea. 
Minimal, stripped back. Nothing else in the frame 
but the bag, the stone, and the sea. Maximum simplicity.

Lighting: Dusk — soft residual ambient light, 
no direct source. Subtle blue tint beginning to appear 
but the bag retains its warmth. 
Sweet melancholy.

Style: Natural pack shot. The bag as sculpture.
Palette: deep blue sea against warm sand (#F5E6D3). 
Subtle 35mm film grain fading into softness.
References: Early Jacquemus minimalism, 
product-as-sculpture aesthetic.
Keywords: minimalism, sculpture, sea, 
dusk, memory, desire crystallized.

Music: Final note — the music resolves, 
one last guitar note held, then silence.
SFX: Progressive silence. Calm sea only.
Ambient: Only the sea. 
The world stops. Slow fade to black.
Emotion: Crystallized desire, purchase intention.

No voiceover. No text overlay. No jump cuts. 
No artificial lighting. Photorealistic. 9: 16 vertical.
Fade to black over 1.5 seconds for end card transition.
